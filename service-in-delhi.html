Why is Labor Law Important for Companies?

Labor law plays an important role to companies because it ensures compliance to employment laws, helps in avoiding legal disputes, and promotes a fair healthy workplace. By following these laws, companies protect themselves from potential legal issues, enhance employee satisfaction and productivity, and build a positive workplace culture.
