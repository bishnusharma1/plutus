Accounting & Assurance

Accounting and assurance involve holding correct financial records, reviewing compliance with the regulatory standards, and ascertaining whether such records and associated reports indicate the objectivity, reliability, and integrity of financial information for prudent decision-making and stakeholder confidence.

Payroll & labour laws

Payroll and Labour laws are important in ensuring smooth employee management and legal compliance. With payroll processing and compliance with various PF, ESI, and other statutory requirements, businesses will maintain complete transparency, avoid penalties, and gain employee trust. And here at Plutus, we offer end-to-end payroll and Labour law solutions, including:

Implementation & processing Litigation support Labour laws & compliance Function outsource Employee engagement

Investment support

Investment support covers a range of services designed to assist the investor or businessman in making well-informed financial decisions. In order to maximize investment through the assessment of investment possibilities, their risks, and their strategic alignment, it entails due diligence, pre-investment review, portfolio management, and transaction structuring.

Due diligence Investor relations management Portfolio management services Startup consulting Pre-investment reviews Transaction structuring

Compliance & regulatory

Compliance & regulatory filings involve all those submissions a business must make to government bodies, regulatory agencies, and other authorities to ascertain if they are in compliance with legal and industry standards. Such submissions include tax-related, corporate governance, Labour laws, environmental regulations, and many more.

More importantly, compliance & regulatory filings are very essential for companies to remain in legal standing and away from unnecessary penalties or lawsuits.

Taxation services Litigation and assessment Corporate legal matters Compliance & regulatory filings Liquidation and winding up Compliance audits

Advisory & management consultancy services

Advisory & management consulting services help businesses make better decisions, improve efficiency in operations, and ultimately grow better. These services include strategic advice, business planning, risk management, and process optimization. This way, by dealing with current practices, identifying opportunities, improving efficiency and reducing risks, and developing strategies for long-term success, organizations can navigate challenges successfully. Plutus shall provide the expertise towards this end.
