Why We?

Founded in 2017, Plutus may be young, but we’re backed by over 50 years of wisdom thanks to our roots with Suresh Chandra & Associates. This journey has allowed us to inherit deep financial expertise and a legacy of trusted service. We've gathered a great deal of financial expertise and a track record for reliable service, using the best techniques and modern strategy for today's problems.

Being a full service chartered accountancy firm with sound principles and values we felt the need to re-born in this new era to be able to work closest to our clients and businesses of this century and hence decided to segregate the advisory & consulting arm to work with the most amazing CXO’s and teams under the brand name Plutus.
