Plutus’ Refund Notice

Last updated on 29 January 2024

Refund Policy

At Plutus Co., we strive to deliver high-quality Virtual CFO (VCFO) services to meet the needs of our clients. We are committed to ensuring your satisfaction. However, we understand that there may be situations where a refund request is necessary. This Refund Policy explains the conditions under which refunds may be granted and the process for making such a request.

1. Eligibility for Refunds

Refunds are available under the following conditions:

  • Service Non-Delivery: If we fail to deliver the agreed-upon services within the specified time frame, you are eligible for a full refund.
  • Service Dissatisfaction: If you are dissatisfied with the service quality, we will work with you to resolve the issue. If a mutually satisfactory resolution cannot be achieved, a partial refund may be considered based on the services rendered.
  • Duplicate Payment: If you have been charged twice for the same service, we will refund the duplicate payment in full.

2. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will not be issued in the following situations:

  • Completed Services: Once services have been rendered or delivered according to the agreement, they are considered final and non-refundable.
  • Client-Initiated Termination: If you choose to terminate the services prematurely for reasons not related to service quality or non-delivery, no refund will be issued for the remaining period of the contract.
  • Change of Mind: Refunds are not available for changes in business needs or situations where you no longer require the service.

3. Refund Request Process

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

  • Contact Us: Email our support team at info@plutusco.com with your name, contact information, service details, and reason for the refund request.
  • Review Process: We will review your refund request and respond within 5 business days.
  • Resolution: If your request meets the conditions outlined in this policy, we will process your refund within 5 business days.

4. Partial Refunds

In some cases, a partial refund may be applicable based on the services already rendered. We will review the work completed and deduct fees for the services provided before issuing a refund.

5. Refund Method

Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used for the purchase. Please note that the processing time may vary depending on your bank or payment provider.
