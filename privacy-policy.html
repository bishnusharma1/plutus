1. Information We Collect

    a) Personal Data

    • Your name

    We may also collect data about how you interact with our Site ("Usage Data"). This includes:

    • Your IP address

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide and maintain our services
  • Personalize your experience

  • Notify you about changes to our services

3. Sharing Your Data

We only share your information in the following cases:

  • Service Providers: Third parties who assist us with our services may have access to your data but only to the extent necessary to perform their roles.
