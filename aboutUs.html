About Plutus

What started as a journey in 1972 has reached a milestone where we look back and can proudly offer to stand firmly for our clients. Being a full service chartered accountancy firm with sound principles and values we felt the need to re-born in this new era to be able to work closest to our clients and businesses of this century and hence decided to segregate the advisory & consulting arm to work with the most amazing CXO’s and teams under the brand name Plutus. This would give us an opportunity to innovate and digitise our services to meet the client needs faster and better than earlier and create more value for them.

Our Mission

Our journey has been all about leading by example and setting up new industry benchmarks on quality standards keeping service delivery at fore. Our zeal to explore new technologies that bring out efficiencies in whatever we do enables us to closely track the deliverables till its conclusion to the client's satisfaction. Strong adherence to the service level agreements (SLA) and regular updates has ensured that our client’s are able to monitor the results that matter the most. Assuring our clients that we have their back while they focus on growth is what we aim for.

Our Values

Our values are an embodiment of our working style and reflects in our services. Our ethical leadership fosters a culture of integrity, professionalism, and client first values in the organisation. This not only builds credibility and trust but also cultivates a positive work environment where individuals feel valued and empowered to make principled decisions.

We encourage a culture of innovation to drive growth and competitiveness by promoting creativity and experimentation. While we continue to make use of innovative tools and latest technology to render our services in a meaningful manner we make sure to measure the efficiency and effectiveness of such processes to make results clearly visible.

Our values combined with teamwork drives the Excellence in the organisation.

Our Strengths

We take pride in what we do and how we do it. Quality is directly related to the technical prowess a service provider possesses in their field. Our leadership consists of qualified subject matter experts across all functional areas to render services un- compromised. Our leadership and team of experts comprises Chartered Accountants, Company Secretaries, Lawyers, Advocates, MBA graduates and other professionals holding certifications in various subjects. The leadership is supported by a sufficient number of team members at all times to ensure smoother & timely execution of tasks. Thorough understanding of the subjects and working closely with the CXO’s ensures we work as an integral part of any organisation. Impeccable interpersonal skills demonstrated by our leadership is our key to lead any organisation from the front keeping its financial & regulatory pillars protected.

Our Culture

The culture of any organisation makes or breaks an organisation. Work is essential but so is play. Maintaining a healthy and respectful work environment is one of our employees prerogatives. Our flexible leave policies, work from home policy including others provide the much needed flexibility to employees to easily navigate through their personal and professional lives. Our annual off-site trips and interim day fun-trips make sure employees feel valued and rejuvenated after every short powerplay and are ready to take the work front with full zeal to meet the next set of newer challenges. The employee retention being higher than 5 years is sufficient proof of the relationship enjoyed by the organisation and employees resulting in higher growth for both the organisation and the employees.

Our Team

We are a group of innovative, experienced, and proficient teams. You will love to collaborate with us.
